E-Mobility Trials

Feb - March

Details

Theme

Make a short presentation about the current challenges in the electric mobility industry in India. The challenges should be covering all possible kinds such as technical, commercial, policy, supply chain, electricity, charging related etc. even from the daily EV driver/passenger point-of-view. Think of all kinds of possible challenges in the EV industry in India today and list them out in the form of a short presentation. For references, there are many reports online which you can read and find more about.


Rules

  • Plagiarism is not allowed.
  • Presentation should not exceed 25 slides.
  • Decision made by the judge is final.


Judging Criteria

  • Thoroughness of the Research: 20%
  • Originality: 30%
  • Offline Presentation + Communication skills: 50%
  • Extra points will be given to the team which does the most comprehensive list of challenges in the presentation. Good online research can get you there.


Resources

  • To understand the current challenges, one has to understand what is currently happening in India in the e-mobility industry broadly. Some resources where you can start are:
    • https://e-amrit.niti.gov.in/home
    • https://www.ceew.in/cef/tools_and_dashboards/electric-mobility/home
    • https://bolt.earth/blog/future-electric-vehicles-india
