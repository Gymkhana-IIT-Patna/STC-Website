EZDues

Feb-March

Details

Problem Statement

Develop a “No Dues Portal” for IIT P students graduating, which will cover the due clearance process of all the different sections of the college seamlessly. The participants of the project will be the students, admins, and different departments where the student needs to apply for No dues.

  1. Students: Students will have their own individual account on the website, where students in need for “No Dues” can request to the concerned department through the website.
  2. Departments: There are various departments where the student needs to apply for “No Dues”. A list of such concerned departments at IIT Patna are:
    • Hostel
    • Sports
    • TPC (Training and Placement Cell)
    • Academic Section
    • Admin Section
    • Accounts Section
    • Computer Center
    • Department
    • Civil Workshop
    • Mechanical Workshop
    • Electrical Workshop
    These departments can add any fine imposed on any student, maintain a digital record and issue “No Dues” certificates electronically. On a student's request of “No Dues”, they can then approve/reject the request, based on any pending dues by the student(which will be maintained on the website).
  3. Admin: The role of the admin will be to create accounts for incoming students, close records of graduated students and resolve any issues regarding forgotten username or password.


Feature Guidelines

  • Students
    • Outlook-based user authentication.
    • Dashboard with quick details of dues with deadlines.
    • Clearance checklist - a comprehensive list outlining all necessary clearances for students.
    • Feature for uploading payment proofs of dues by students.
    • Search and filter functionality for different departments and due amounts.
    • Deadline Tracking: Clear display of deadlines for clearance submission.
  • Departments
    • Dashboard with quick details of dues the department owes from students.
    • Adding dues of any student with details like amount, reason, damage proofs, etc., for fines.
    • Payment proof verification and approval.
    • Search and filter students based on how much due each student has.
    • Auto approval: On request of “No Dues” by a student, the system will auto approve the request (after a deadline) when there are no existing dues in the record if no action is taken by the concerned department.
  • Admins
    • User authentication (this will be the master account).
    • Bulk registration through the admin pane by passing an Excel sheet or a CSV and mailing their credentials in case of new admissions.
    • Super admin panel: Easy maintenance of accounts by Admin, creation of new accounts in case of incoming batches, and closing accounts of graduated users.


Bonus Features

  1. Option to pay the fine through the portal itself.
  2. Bulk registration through the admin pane by passing an Excel sheet or a CSV and mailing their credentials in case of new admissions.
  3. Adding fine for multiple students through Excel sheet or a CSV.
  4. Email reminders to students for dues with approaching deadlines.


Rules

  1. Don’t use any kind of paid/cloud-based products (like AWS, Google Cloud, MongoDB Atlas, Azure, Firebase) in your solution.
  2. Try to use scalable database services like MySQL, SQLite.
  3. Plagiarism is not allowed. You can use open-source licensed code, however.
  4. Decision made by judges is final.


Judging Criteria

The evaluation will be on the basis of:

  1. UI/UX - 10%
  2. Ease of Use - 10%
  3. Functionality - 30%
  4. Scalability and Maintainability - 20%
  5. Technical Complexity - 30%
