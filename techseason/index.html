Grand Championship'24

Jan to April
The Grand Championship at IIT Patna is an annual tech event where students are divided into three houses - Draco, Cassiopeia and Pegasus, engaging in competitions and workshops organized by various technical clubs.
Featuring it's two main components:
  • Tech Season An extensive series of fests organized by diverse technical clubs, encompassing a range of events and workshops.
  • ICTC (Intra College Technical Championship) - the pinnacle of the event, a problem statement-based challenge following the INTER-IIT model, aimed at enhancing the institute's standing and fostering a culture of continuous growth and innovation.
Gear up for IIT Patna's Grand Championship! Compete in Tech Season events and showcase your skills in the challenging ICTC, shaping the event's dynamic culture.
